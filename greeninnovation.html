GREEN INNOVATION

Green Innovation reflects the broader implications of technological inventions in sustainable practices and envisions the cultural shift towards eco-friendly lifestyles.

Green innovation is any form of invention that helps develop essential goods, services, or procedures that minimize environmental damage and degradation while also making the best use of available natural resources. These days, innovation of this kind becomes vital as it directs the wise use of natural resources to enhance human welfare. Additionally, developing and implementing modifications to goods and manufacturing procedures may support sustainable development.
